## Overview

This article collection explores what it means to do authentic creative work in an age of AI collaboration. Starting with theoretical questions about algorithmic performance and culminating in practical frameworks, these pieces trace a journey from skepticism through implementation to new paradigms for evaluating creative partnerships.

<details>
<summary><strong>ğŸ“– More Context</strong> (click to expand)</summary>

This collection emerged from real experience leading design teams through AI tool adoption in 2024. Rather than treating AI as simply another tool, these articles grapple with deeper questions: What makes work "yours"? How do we evaluate value when productivity metrics miss the point? When does AI collaboration enhance versus diminish creative authenticity?

The articles are designed to be read in sequence but work independently. They progress from identifying problems (algorithmic theatre, measurement paradoxes) through practical implementation (collaboration crash course) to real-world application (new venue case study).
