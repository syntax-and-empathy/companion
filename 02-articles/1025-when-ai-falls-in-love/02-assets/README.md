## Overview

This directory houses all visual assets specifically curated for the article "When AI Falls in Love." These assets include critical screenshots demonstrating AI interactions and featured images designed for optimal presentation across various platforms. They are integral to clarifying the article's narrative and reinforcing its key points on AI collaboration and design leadership.

<details>
<summary><strong>About This Content</strong> (click to expand)</summary>

### Purpose & Context

This collection of images serves to enrich the reader's experience of "When AI Falls in Love." It includes screenshots that provide direct visual evidence of AI model behavior discussed in the article, as well as distinct large and small featured images optimized for social sharing and article previews. These assets ensure the article is both visually engaging and effectively communicates its insights.

### Background

The assets were created during the development of the "When AI Falls in Love" article to visually articulate the concepts and examples presented. The Perplexity AI screenshots, for instance, directly support the discussion around AI's capacity for 'empathy' or 'love' as simulated responses, while the featured images establish the article's visual identity.

</details>
