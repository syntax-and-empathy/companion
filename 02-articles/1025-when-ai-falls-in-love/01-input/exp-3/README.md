## Overview

This directory captures the raw input from a series of AI experiments conducted to explore the subtle art of 'anticipation design' within user interfaces. Specifically, it focuses on the hypothetical scenario of introducing an 8-second delay before a dashboard export to build user excitement and perceived value. The files contain direct outputs from ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, offering a comparative look at how different large language models interpret and expand upon this unique design prompt.
